My Favorite Holiday Destination: Bali

Bali, also known as the Island of the Gods, is a popular holiday destination in Indonesia. It is famous for its beautiful beaches, scenic landscapes, and rich culture.

  1. Ubud Monkey Forest - a natural forest sanctuary that is home to over 600 long-tailed macaques.
  2. Uluwatu Temple - a stunning cliff-top temple that offers breathtaking views of the Indian Ocean.
  3. Tegalalang Rice Terrace - a beautiful rice terrace that is popular for its picturesque scenery.
  4. Kuta Beach - a famous beach that is known for its white sand, surfing, and nightlife.
  5. Bali Safari and Marine Park - a family-friendly park that features a wide variety of animals, including tigers, elephants, and orangutans.
  • Nasi Goreng - a fried rice dish that is usually served with chicken or shrimp.
  • Mie Goreng - a stir-fried noodle dish that is similar to Nasi Goreng.
  • Sate - grilled skewered meat that is usually served with peanut sauce.
  • Gado-Gado - a salad dish that is made with boiled vegetables, tofu, and peanut sauce.
  • Babi Guling - a Balinese-style roasted pig dish that is often served during special occasions.
